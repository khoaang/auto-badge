Made by Blake Haug and Khoa Nguyen

In order to print, you need to create a spreadsheet with a names column and a google drive link column.

Make sure the images are viewable by anyone that has the link.
This is most easily done by making the entire google drive folder accessible.

Then, download the csv file of the spreadsheet.

That's all! Just upload the csv under the spreadsheet options.
